About me...

Hi! I am Ruth García AKA: pr00thmatic. I love gamedev!

I started as a 2D artist in the GGJ2014 and then jumped right into javascript and phaser. When the opportunity to work as a freelancer game developer came to me, I was required to learn Unity, Arduino and Playcanvas.

I love learning to work with new engines and technology for any gamedev-related thing.

I have a programmer's heart, it's my favorite thing in the world. I've been in love with videogames since I was a toddler, from playing them to develop them. That’s the reason why I also learned to be a generalist, it's amazing all the process involved on making games.

So... by all means! please feel free to contact me in any social network to chat about game development.

I am Bolivian/Guatemalan developer so it’s my pleasure to work with you, even if my grammar isn’t perfect.
